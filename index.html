About

I am the founder and CEO of Holistic Rehabilitation Services Private Limited. The business started 1/2 year ago as a two-person operation in Peshawar and has since grown to employ over 30 talented people. We have an easy management structure and our customers are Mostly from Peshawar. In 2021, we increased our customer base by 30% and expanded into new markets.

Holistic Rehabilitation Services Private Limited specialize in providing Rehabilitation services to peoples. Our goal is to make peoples independent from the disability.
